### 2025

#### Neurodivergent Biomimetic Multi-Agent Architecture (November 2025)

**Full Title:** *Neurodivergent Biomimetic Multi-Agent Architecture: Direct Silicon Translation of ADHD/Autistic Cognition Under Enforced Scarcity*

**Author:** Domenic Garza  
**Date:** November 23, 2025  
**Status:** Pre-print  
**Directory:** `2025_neurodivergent_biomimetic_architecture/`

**Abstract:** This paper discloses the first known computational organism constructed as a direct 1:1 translation of a living neurodivergent (ADHD/autistic) human brain onto consumer gaming laptops operating under deliberate resource scarcity. Two U.S. provisional patents were filed on November 23, 2025.
