## **Customization Guidelines**

### **For Different Career Stages**

**Entry-Level / Recent Graduate**
* Emphasize emerging capabilities at highest tiers
* "Developing proficiency at Create tier"
* Focus on academic projects demonstrating progression

**Mid-Level Engineer**
* Emphasize consistent operation at Analyze/Evaluate
* "Regular operation at Create tier on [specific projects]"
* Balance between demonstrated capability and growth potential

**Senior Engineer / Architect**
* Emphasize sustained Create tier work
* "Consistent operation at highest cognitive level"
* Focus on impact and innovation

### **For Different Industries**

**Startups / Innovation-Focused**
* Heavy emphasis on Create tier
* Highlight autonomous problem-solving
* Focus on novel solution generation

**Enterprise / Established Companies**
* Balanced presentation across all three tiers
* Emphasize evaluation and analysis for existing systems
* Show create tier for new initiatives

**Academia / Research**
* Maximum emphasis on Create tier
* Connect to research contribution potential
* Highlight novel framework development

**Consulting**
* Strong Evaluate tier emphasis
* Show analytical capabilities
* Demonstrate synthesis across domains
