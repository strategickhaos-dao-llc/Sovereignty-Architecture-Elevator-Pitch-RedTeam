## Why These Papers Matter

Genomic data is the ultimate personal data:
- **CRISPR** enables precise genome editing
- **Privacy** is critical for genetic data
- **Ethics** must guide powerful technologies
- **Discrimination** risks are real and present
- **Sovereignty** over our genetic code is fundamental

Essential for:
- Understanding biotech capabilities
- Genetic privacy protection
- Ethical gene editing governance
- Biosafety considerations
- Personal genomic sovereignty

## Key Ethical Issues

### Germline Editing
- **Heritable changes** affect future generations
- **Consent** of the unborn
- **Equity** in access to enhancements

### Privacy & Discrimination
- **Re-identification** from genomic data
- **Insurance** and employment discrimination
- **Family implications** of genetic data

### Biosafety & Biosecurity
- **Gene drives** can spread uncontrollably
- **Synthetic organisms** pose ecological risks
- **Dual-use** research concerns
