# Category 05: Neuroscience & Collective Intelligence

This category contains 10 papers on brain networks, collective intelligence, and emergent cognition.

## Papers Included

1. **The Brain's Default Network** - Raichle et al.
   - Neural basis of introspection
   - Default mode network

2. **Neural Basis of Consciousness** - Various authors
   - How consciousness emerges from neural activity
   - Integrated information theory

3. **Collective Intelligence Factor** - Woolley et al. (2010)
   - "c factor" in group intelligence
   - What makes groups smart

4. **Mirror Neurons and Imitation Learning** - Rizzolatti et al.
   - Neural basis of empathy
   - Learning through observation

5. **Human Connectome Project** - Various authors
   - Mapping brain connectivity
   - Network structure of thought

6. **Predictive Coding in the Brain** - Friston et al.
   - Brain as prediction machine
   - Hierarchical processing

7. **Free Energy Principle** - Karl Friston
   - Unified brain theory
   - Minimizing surprise

8. **Bayesian Brain** - Various authors
   - Brain as Bayesian inference engine
   - Probabilistic cognition

9. **Scale-Free Brain Networks** - Bullmore & Sporns
   - Network topology of brain
   - Small-world properties

10. **Wisdom of Crowds** - Surowiecki research
    - Collective decision making
    - Emergent intelligence

## Why These Papers Matter

Understanding collective intelligence is key to building swarm systems:
- **Brain networks** inspire distributed architectures
- **Collective intelligence** emerges from interaction
- **Predictive coding** enables efficient agents
- **Scale-free networks** enable robust coordination

Essential for:
- Multi-agent systems
- Swarm intelligence
- Emergent behavior
- Collective decision making
