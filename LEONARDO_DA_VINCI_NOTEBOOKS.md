### Why Mirror Writing?

- **Leonardo was left-handed.** Writing left-to-right with a quill would have smudged the ink across the page (and his hand).

He still used it consistently for almost everything personal. Some scholars think it was also a subtle way to discourage casual copying in an era with no copyright.
