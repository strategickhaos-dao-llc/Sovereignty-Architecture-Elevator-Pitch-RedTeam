## Verification Process

### Step 1: Document Collection
- Gather all required documents
- Ensure documents are current (within 90 days for some items)
- Verify all documents have proper signatures

### Step 2: Document Review
- Check for completeness
- Verify EIN matches across documents
- Confirm entity name consistency
- Validate officer/member information

### Step 3: Submission Preparation
- Create digital copies (high resolution)
- Organize documents by category
- Prepare document checklist
- Schedule appointment with Navy Federal
