# Interview Question Handling Departments

This directory contains specialized departments designed to handle different categories of interview questions, particularly focused on Navy Federal Credit Union business account eligibility and alternative banking solutions.

## ğŸ›ï¸ Department Structure

### [01. Membership Eligibility Department](01_membership_eligibility.md)
**Primary Function:** Determines if applicant qualifies for Navy Federal Credit Union membership

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Verify military service status
- Check DOD employment/contractor status
- Validate family member connections
- Make initial eligibility determination
- Route to appropriate next department

**Questions Handled:**
- Do you qualify for Navy Federal membership?
- Are you military/veteran/DOD employee?
- Do family members have Navy Federal accounts?

---

### [02. Business Account Services Department](02_business_account_services.md)
**Primary Function:** Provides information on Navy Federal business products and services

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Explain available business banking products
- Detail business credit card options
- Describe business loan products
- List supported entity types (LLC, Corp, Nonprofit, etc.)
- Outline account features and benefits

**Questions Handled:**
- What business accounts does Navy Federal offer?
- Can I get a business credit card?
- Does Navy Federal support LLCs and nonprofits?
- What business loan products are available?

---

### [03. Document Requirements Department](03_document_requirements.md)
**Primary Function:** Manages documentation requirements for business account opening

**Key Responsibilities:**
- List required documents for LLCs
- List required documents for 501(c)(3) nonprofits
- Verify document completeness
- Guide document preparation
- Conduct document gap analysis

**Questions Handled:**
- What documents do I need for my LLC?
- What's required for nonprofit accounts?
- Do I have all necessary documentation?
- What is a corporate resolution?

**Entity-Specific Checklists:**
- Wyoming LLC (Strategickhaos DAO LLC)
- 501(c)(3) Nonprofit (ValorYield Engine)
- Corporations
- Sole Proprietorships

---

### [04. Alternative Banking Solutions Department](04_alternative_banking_solutions.md)
**Primary Function:** Provides comprehensive banking alternatives for non-eligible applicants

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Recommend alternative banking institutions
- Compare features and fees
- Design banking architecture strategies
- Support both for-profit and nonprofit entities
- Guide implementation of alternative solutions

**Questions Handled:**
- What if I don't qualify for Navy Federal?
- What are the best alternatives?
- Which banks are good for startups/LLCs/nonprofits?
- How do I build a multi-bank strategy?

**Banking Solutions Covered:**
- Traditional Banks (Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo)
- Digital Banks (Mercury, Relay, Novo, Thread Bank)
- Fintech Solutions (BlueVine, Capital One Spark)

---

### [05. Eligibility Verification Department](05_eligibility_verification.md)
**Primary Function:** Conducts detailed verification of Navy Federal eligibility

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Conduct comprehensive eligibility screening
- Verify military service documentation
- Validate DOD employment/contractor status
- Confirm family member connections
- Handle edge cases and special circumstances
- Make final eligibility determinations

**Questions Handled:**
- What specific proof do I need for eligibility?
- Does my family connection qualify me?
- I'm a government contractor - am I eligible?
- What if my parent was in the military?

**Verification Areas:**
- Military service records (DD-214, military ID)
- DOD employment verification
- DOD contractor documentation
- Family member Navy Federal accounts
- Household member verification
