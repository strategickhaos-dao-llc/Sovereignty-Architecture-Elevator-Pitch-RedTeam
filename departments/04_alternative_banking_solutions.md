# Alternative Banking Solutions Department

## Purpose
Provides comprehensive banking alternatives for clients who do not qualify for Navy Federal membership or prefer other banking options.

## Key Questions Handled

### 1. What if I don't qualify for Navy Federal?
**Q: What are the alternative banking options for my business entities?**

## Alternative Banking Institutions

### Traditional Banks (Open to Any Business with EIN)

#### Tier 1: Major National Banks
- **Chase Business Banking**
  - Full suite of business products
  - Nationwide branch network
  - Strong mobile/online platform
  - Business credit cards with rewards
  
- **Bank of America Business Advantage**
  - Comprehensive business solutions
  - Merchant services
  - Business loans and lines of credit
  - International banking capabilities

- **Wells Fargo Business Banking**
  - Established business banking platform
  - Multiple account types
  - Treasury management services
  - Business credit products

#### Tier 2: Digital-First Business Banks
- **Mercury**
  - Modern digital banking
  - VC-backed startup focus
  - Free business checking
  - Excellent API integrations
  - FDIC insured through partner banks

- **Relay**
  - Multiple checking accounts (up to 20)
  - No monthly fees
  - Great for cash flow management
  - Integrates with accounting software

- **Novo**
  - No monthly fees
  - Free business checking
  - Integrated invoicing
  - FDIC insured
  - Mobile-first design

- **Thread Bank + Sequence**
  - Already in use by client
  - Specialized for startups and SMBs
  - Integrated financial tools

#### Tier 3: Fintech Solutions
- **BlueVine**
  - Business checking with 2.0% interest
  - Line of credit available
  - Invoice factoring
  - Fast funding

- **Capital One Spark**
  - Business checking and savings
  - Cash back credit cards
  - No foreign transaction fees
  - Strong mobile app

## Implementation Process

### Phase 1: Research & Selection
1. Review banking needs for each entity
2. Compare features and fees
3. Select 2-3 banking partners
4. Prepare documentation

### Phase 2: Account Opening
1. Submit applications online
2. Upload required documents
3. Complete identity verification
4. Activate accounts

### Phase 3: Setup & Integration
1. Link to accounting software
2. Set up online bill pay
3. Apply for business credit cards
4. Configure alerts and notifications
