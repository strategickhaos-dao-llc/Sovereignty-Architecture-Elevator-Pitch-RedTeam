## Scientific Foundations

### Photosynthesis Quantum Effects
- **Key Paper**: Engel et al. (2007), "Evidence for wavelike energy transfer through quantum coherence in photosynthetic systems"
- **Finding**: Room-temperature quantum coherence in light-harvesting proteins
- **Relevance**: Proves quantum effects can be maintained in warm, noisy environments

### Grotthuss Mechanism
- **Key Paper**: Agmon (1995), "The Grotthuss mechanism"
- **Finding**: Proton transport through structural rearrangement in water chains
- **Relevance**: Model for error correction through dynamic structural adaptation

### Quantum Error Correction
- **Key Paper**: Shor (1995), "Scheme for reducing decoherence in quantum computer memory"
- **Finding**: Quantum error correction through redundancy and measurement
- **Comparison**: VFASP complements traditional QEC with bio-inspired mechanisms
