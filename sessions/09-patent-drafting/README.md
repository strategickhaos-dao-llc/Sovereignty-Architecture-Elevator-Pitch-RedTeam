## Legal Considerations

### Patent Strategy
- **Defensive**: Prevent predatory patents on our innovations
- **Offensive**: License technology for revenue
- **Public**: Defensive publication for non-core innovations

### Trade Secrets vs Patents
- **Patent**: Core architectural innovations (VFASP, exports)
- **Trade Secret**: Specific implementation optimizations
- **Open Source**: Non-differentiating components

### Geographic Coverage
- **Priority 1**: USA (USPTO)
- **Priority 2**: Europe (EPO)
- **Priority 3**: China, Japan, Korea (tech markets)
