## ðŸš¨ CRITICAL WARNINGS

### The 12-Month Deadline

**THIS IS NOT NEGOTIABLE.**

You have EXACTLY 12 months from filing date to:
- Convert to non-provisional, OR
- File PCT application, OR
- Let provisional expire

**There are NO extensions.**  
**There is NO "oops I forgot."**  
**Missing this deadline = start over with new priority date.**

Set multiple reminders. Take this seriously.

### Micro Entity Status

**Don't lose it accidentally.**

Common ways people lose micro entity status:
- Assigning patent rights to VC-backed company
- Selling company to large acquirer mid-prosecution
- Licensing to enterprise customer without proper agreement
- Income exceeding 3x median household

**If you lose status:** Notify USPTO immediately and pay higher fees going forward.

### Public Disclosure

**After filing, you can disclose publicly.**

Before filing, public disclosure can destroy patentability in many countries (Europe, Asia).

US has 1-year grace period, but other countries don't.

**Best practice:** File first, disclose second.
