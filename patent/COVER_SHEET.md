## NOTES FOR FILING

### Priority Date

This provisional application establishes a priority date upon filing. The applicant has 12 months from the filing date to:
- Convert to a non-provisional application
- File a non-provisional application claiming priority to this provisional
- File a Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT) application
- Allow the provisional to expire (if protection is not desired)

### Provisional Application Characteristics

**Important:** Provisional applications are NOT examined by the USPTO. They simply establish a filing date and priority claim for future applications.

Provisional applications:
- Do not require claims (but this application includes them for clarity)
- Do not require an oath or declaration (but this application includes one)
- Do not require an Information Disclosure Statement (IDS)
- Are not published by the USPTO
- Automatically expire 12 months after filing

### Publication and Pending Status

Upon filing this provisional application:
- You may use the term "Patent Pending"
- The application number will be provided by USPTO
- The application remains confidential (not published)
- No patent rights are granted until a non-provisional application is filed and approved
