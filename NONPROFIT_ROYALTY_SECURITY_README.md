### Glossary of Key Terms

**Anti-SLAPP**: Anti-Strategic Lawsuit Against Public Participation laws that protect against frivolous lawsuits

**D&O Insurance**: Directors and Officers liability insurance

**UBIT**: Unrelated Business Income Tax - tax on nonprofit income from activities unrelated to exempt purpose

**Royalty**: Payment for use of intellectual property

**Reserve Fund**: Savings set aside for emergencies or specific purposes

**Fiduciary Duty**: Legal obligation to act in the best interest of the organization

**Indemnification**: Agreement to compensate for losses or damages

**Risk Transfer**: Shifting risk to another party through insurance or contract
