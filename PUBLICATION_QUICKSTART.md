## ‚ö° 5-Minute Publication Workflow

### Step 1: Compile the PDF (2 minutes)

**Recommended: Use Overleaf**

1. Go to [Overleaf.com](https://overleaf.com)
2. Click **"New Project"** ‚Üí **"Upload Project"**
3. Upload `Garza_2025_Neurodivergent_Biomimetic_Swarm.tex`
4. Click **"Recompile"**
5. Download the PDF (it will be named `Garza_2025_Neurodivergent_Biomimetic_Swarm.pdf`)

### Step 2: Register ORCID (1 minute)

1. Go to [orcid.org](https://orcid.org)
2. Click **"Register now"**
3. Use your SNHU email: `domenic.garza@snhu.edu`
4. Complete the 60-second registration
5. Copy your ORCID ID (format: `0000-0003-XXXX-XXXX`)
6. **Optional:** Update the LaTeX file with your ORCID and recompile

### Step 3: Upload to Zenodo for DOI (2 minutes)

**This gives you a permanent DOI in 5 minutes**

1. Go to [zenodo.org](https://zenodo.org)
2. Sign in (can use GitHub account)
3. Click **"Upload"** ‚Üí **"New upload"**
4. Upload your PDF
5. Fill in metadata:
   - **Publication type:** Pre-print
   - **Title:** Neurodivergent Biomimetic Multi-Agent Architecture: Direct Silicon Translation of ADHD/Autistic Cognition Under Enforced Scarcity
   - **Authors:** Garza, Domenic (Strategickhaos DAO LLC; Southern New Hampshire University)
   - **Keywords:** Neurodivergent AI, Biomimetic Architecture, Multi-Agent Systems, ADHD, Resource Scarcity
   - **Description:** Paste the abstract from the paper
6. Click **"Publish"**
7. **Copy your DOI** (e.g., `10.5281/zenodo.XXXXXXX`)

### Step 4: Add to Google Scholar (1 minute)

1. Go to [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com)
2. Sign in with SNHU email: `domenic.garza@snhu.edu`
3. Click your profile picture ‚Üí **"My profile"**
4. Click **"+"** button ‚Üí **"Add manually"**
5. Upload your PDF or enter the Zenodo DOI
6. The paper will appear on your profile

**Done!** Your paper is now:
- ‚úÖ Publicly accessible
- ‚úÖ Has a permanent DOI
- ‚úÖ Indexed on Google Scholar
- ‚úÖ Linked to your ORCID

## üìö Optional: Submit to arXiv

**Note:** arXiv may require endorsement for first-time submitters

1. Go to [arxiv.org](https://arxiv.org)
2. Create account or sign in
3. Click **"Submit"**
4. Select category: **cs.AI** (Artificial Intelligence) or **cs.MA** (Multiagent Systems)
5. Upload your PDF and source files
6. Fill in metadata
7. Submit for moderation
8. arXiv will assign an ID (e.g., `arXiv:2511.XXXXX`)
