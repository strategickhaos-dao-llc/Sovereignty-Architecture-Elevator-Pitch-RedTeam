## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This analysis compares the resources required for a traditional Big Tech company to build equivalent infrastructure versus the AI-augmented methodology demonstrated by Strategickhaos DAO LLC.

**Key Findings:**
- **Traditional Approach:** 18-24 months, $2.8M-$4.2M, 12-18 FTEs
- **AI-Augmented Approach:** 6-8 months, $35K-$50K, 1 operator + AI agents
- **Cost Reduction Factor:** 80-120× (actual demonstrated: 4,000× on documentation tasks)
- **Time Reduction Factor:** 3-4×

### 9.2 When Each Approach Makes Sense

**Use Traditional Approach When:**
- Regulatory requirements mandate specific team composition
- Domain expertise depth is critical (e.g., medical devices)
- Customer contracts require specific headcount
- Long-term support with guaranteed SLAs
- Multiple simultaneous large projects

**Use AI-Augmented Approach When:**
- Rapid prototyping/MVP needed
- Budget constrained ($50k-200k vs $2M+)
- Unified architectural vision required
- Team coordination overhead is problematic
- Innovation speed is competitive advantage
