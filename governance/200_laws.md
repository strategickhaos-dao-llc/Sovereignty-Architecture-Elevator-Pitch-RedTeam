### Requirements for Amendment
1. Proposal must be submitted in writing with clear rationale
2. 14-day community review and discussion period
3. Attorney review for legal compliance (where applicable)
4. 75% approval threshold for general laws
5. 90% approval threshold for core laws (#1-#20)
6. 100% approval + attorney certification for Law #15

### Prohibited Amendments
- Cannot reduce ValorYield below 7%
- Cannot eliminate ecosystem support mandate
- Cannot introduce UPL violations
- Cannot compromise sovereignty principles
- Cannot enable personal enrichment over mission
