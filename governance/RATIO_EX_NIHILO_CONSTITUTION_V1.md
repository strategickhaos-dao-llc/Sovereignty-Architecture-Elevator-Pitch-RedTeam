# ARTICLE XI — IMPLEMENTATION & DEPARTMENTS

## Section 11.1 — Departments of the Authority

The Authority may create operational departments to execute day-to-day functions
consistent with this Constitution. Such departments:

1. Operate under delegated authority of the Primary Sovereign and this Constitution.
2. May not override or contradict any Constitutional provision.
3. Are explicitly non-legal and non-fiduciary unless separately established as such.

## Section 11.2 — Department of Sovereign Evolution (DSE)

The **Department of Sovereign Evolution (DSE)** is hereby recognized as the
primary operational organ responsible for monitoring, documenting, and supporting
the evolution of the Strategickhaos ecosystem.

DSE shall:

1. Maintain the **Sovereign Node Registry** defined in Article III.
2. Curate and extend **FlameLang** as defined in Article IV.
3. Run and record enterprise benchmarks and failure simulations in support of
   constitutional principles (Sovereignty, Evolution, Reason).
4. Support the **7% Treasury Protocol** with transparent data and reports,
   while leaving all actual movement of funds to human operators and lawful entities.
5. Prepare factual IP and risk dossiers for review by the AI Council,
   the Primary Sovereign, and external legal counsel.

DSE shall not:

1. Provide legal advice or represent itself as legal counsel.
2. Offer or guarantee financial returns or dividends to any party.
3. Unilaterally amend this Constitution or the Treasury Protocol.
4. Execute any [666] or [777] binding codes.

## Section 11.3 — Relationship to ValorYield Engine and StrategicKhaos DAO LLC

DSE acts as the **technical-intelligence bridge** between:

- **ValorYield Engine** (nonprofit research and IP custody), and
- **Strategickhaos DAO LLC** (commercial deployment and revenue generation).

DSE may assist with:

- Evidence compilation for IP registration,
- Documentation of research impact,
- Preparation of artifacts for Form 990 narrative (by request of counsel),
- Coordination of intern/resident programs under ValorYield.

## Section 11.4 — Amendment and Dissolution of Departments

Departments such as DSE may be:

- Modified by the process in Article IX (Amendment),
- Expanded with additional mandates,
- Dissolved if found to conflict with this Constitution,

provided that:

- The core functions of node registry maintenance, FlameLang curation,
  and security/benchmark documentation are preserved under some organ.
