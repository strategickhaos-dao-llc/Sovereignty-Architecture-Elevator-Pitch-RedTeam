## Article III: Inter-Department Relations

### Section 3.1 - Department Coordination

All departments shall coordinate through established protocols. No department may override the jurisdiction of another without explicit constitutional amendment.

### Section 3.2 - Conflict Resolution

In cases of inter-departmental conflict:
1. The Sovereignty Department serves as final arbiter
2. Decisions must align with constitutional principles
3. All resolutions must be documented and logged

### Section 3.3 - Resource Sharing

Departments shall share resources as needed for operational efficiency, with the Infrastructure Department maintaining the shared resource pool.

---

## Article IV: Amendment Process

### Section 4.1 - Proposal

Any department may propose amendments to this Constitution through the Nonprofit/Legal Department.

### Section 4.2 - Review

Proposed amendments must be reviewed by:
1. The AI Council Department (for technical feasibility)
2. The Sovereignty Department (for constitutional alignment)
3. The Nonprofit/Legal Department (for legal compliance)

### Section 4.3 - Ratification

Amendments are ratified when approved by the authorized signers as defined in the governance framework.
