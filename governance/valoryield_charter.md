This charter establishes the ValorYield charitable contribution program as the sacred 7% heartbeat of the Strategickhaos ecosystem.

---

## Mission Statement

ValorYield represents the commitment to channel a permanent portion of all ecosystem revenues toward causes that save lives, reduce suffering, and honor those who serve.

**The Promise**: Kids with cancer. Doctors saving lives in war zones. Veterans who sacrificed for others. This is the heartbeat that never stops.

---

## Revenue Allocation

### Source
7% of all gross revenues from the Strategickhaos ecosystem, including:
- Software licensing and royalties
- Enterprise seat sales
- Model and algorithm sales
- Security bounties (platform fees)
- Merchandise and branded materials
- Consulting and professional services
- Any other monetization streams

### Status
**IRREVOCABLE**: This allocation cannot be reduced, eliminated, or diverted  
**ON-CHAIN**: Enforced through multi-signature smart contracts  
**ETERNAL**: No sunset clause, continues in perpetuity  
**PUBLIC**: All distributions publicly verifiable on blockchain

## Beneficiary Organizations

### St. Jude Children's Research Hospital (40%)
**Allocation**: 2.8% of gross revenues  
**Mission**: Leading the way the world understands, treats and defeats childhood cancer  
**Why**: Because no child should die in the dawn of life. Because their parents should never receive a bill.  
**Impact**: Every dollar helps ensure families never pay for treatment, travel, housing or food  

### Médecins Sans Frontières / Doctors Without Borders (40%)
**Allocation**: 2.8% of gross revenues  
**Mission**: Providing independent, impartial medical humanitarian assistance  
**Why**: Because doctors who run toward war zones when everyone else runs away deserve unlimited support  
**Impact**: Emergency medical care in conflict zones, epidemics, disasters, and areas excluded from healthcare  

### Veterans Support Organizations (20%)
**Allocation**: 1.4% of gross revenues  
**Mission**: Supporting those who served their communities and countries  
**Selection**: Rotating selection of high-impact veterans organizations  
**Why**: Because those who sacrificed their comfort, safety, and sometimes their wholeness deserve lifelong support  
**Impact**: Mental health services, housing support, job training, community reintegration  

#### Veterans Organization Selection Criteria
Organizations must:
- Have direct impact on veteran wellbeing
- Maintain high efficiency ratios (>75% to programs)
- Demonstrate measurable outcomes
- Serve underserved veteran populations
- Welcome oversight and transparency

**Review Cycle**: Annual community vote on veterans organization selection

## Implementation Architecture

### Smart Contract Structure

**Multi-Signature Wallets**:
- Deployed on Solana mainnet (primary)
- Deployed on Ethereum mainnet (backup)
- Require 3-of-5 signatures for any distribution
- Signers: 2 DAO members + 1 independent auditor + 2 community representatives

**Automatic Splitting**:
- Revenue received → Immediate 7/93 split
- 7% directed to ValorYield escrow wallet
- Monthly batch distributions to beneficiaries
- All transactions publicly verifiable

**Transparency Mechanisms**:
- Public blockchain explorer for all transactions
- Quarterly transparency reports
- Annual third-party audit
- Community dashboard showing real-time balances

### Tax Compliance

**Structure**:
- Distributions structured as charitable contributions from Strategickhaos DAO LLC
- Documentation maintained for IRS 501(c)(3) deduction eligibility
- All beneficiaries verified as qualified 501(c)(3) organizations
- Annual Form 990 disclosures as required

**Professional Support**:
- CPA review of all charitable contribution documentation
- Attorney certification of compliance structure
- Tax professional guidance on deduction optimization

## Distribution Schedule

**Frequency**: Monthly distributions to beneficiary organizations  
**Minimum Threshold**: $1,000 accumulated before distribution (to minimize transaction fees)  
**Emergency Override**: Immediate distribution capability for disaster response  

**Process**:
1. Month-end revenue calculation and 7% allocation
2. Funds transferred to ValorYield escrow wallet
3. 5-day community review period
4. Multi-sig authorization (3-of-5 signatures)
5. Distribution to beneficiaries (40/40/20 split)
6. Public transaction posting and verification
7. Update community dashboard

2. Emergency community notification

5. Restoration of full ValorYield balance
6. Public accountability report
7. System updates to prevent recurrence
