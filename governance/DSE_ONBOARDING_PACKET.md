## 8. FAQ

### General

**Q: How many hours per week are expected?**  
A: This varies by role. Interns typically contribute 5-10 hours. Contributors set their own pace based on capacity.

**Q: Is there compensation?**  
A: Active contributors may receive compensation from the 7% treasury loop. Specifics depend on contribution level and treasury health.

**Q: Can I contribute anonymously?**  
A: Partial anonymity is possible, but identity verification is required for certain privileges. Discuss with CSA.

### Technical

**Q: What programming languages are used?**  
A: Primarily TypeScript/JavaScript, Python, PowerShell, and Bash. FlameLang is our symbolic DSL.

**Q: Do I need specific hardware?**  
A: Basic development requires a modern computer. Node operators have specific hardware requirements.

**Q: How do I set up the development environment?**  
A: Follow the README.md quick start guide. Contact #help if you encounter issues.

### Governance

**Q: How do I propose a new feature?**  
A: Use the RFC template at templates/rfc_template.md and submit to governance/proposals/.

**Q: How long until I can vote?**  
A: Voting rights (L4) require election to Human Council or CSA appointment. Typically 6+ months of active contribution.

**Q: What if I disagree with a decision?**  
A: Raise concerns during comment periods. If still unresolved, escalate through proper channels.
