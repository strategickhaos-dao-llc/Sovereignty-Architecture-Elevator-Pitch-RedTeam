## Governance & Oversight

### Community Review
**Frequency**: Quarterly  
**Process**:
1. Full transparency report of all allocations
2. 14-day community review period
3. Public Q&A session
4. Community vote on any concerns
5. Publication of final report

**Thresholds for Automatic Scrutiny**:
- Single allocation >$5,000
- Cumulative monthly allocations >$25,000
- Any allocation flagged by 3+ community members
- Unusual spending patterns detected by monitoring

### Audit Requirements
- Monthly internal reconciliation by treasury committee
- Quarterly review by governance committee
- Annual comprehensive audit by independent CPA
- Public posting of all reports and findings

### Approval Process

**Tier 1: Auto-Approval (<$500)**
- Electricity/bandwidth reimbursements (documented)

- Process: Automated approval, post-hoc review

**Tier 2: Fast-Track Review ($500-$2,500)**
- GPU purchases for active contributors

- Process: 48-hour review by treasury committee

**Tier 3: Full Review ($2,500-$10,000)**
- Major hardware purchases
- Significant infrastructure investments
- Custom physical rewards
- Process: 7-day review by governance committee + community input

**Tier 4: Community Vote (>$10,000)**
- Major capability expansions
- Large-scale infrastructure projects
- Unusual or precedent-setting expenses
- Process: 14-day community discussion + vote (67% approval required)

## Request Process

### How to Request Support

1. **Submit Request**
   - Use designated community channel or form
   - Include: What you need, why you need it, how it supports the ecosystem
   - Provide cost estimate and alternative options
   - Link to your contributions/activity

2. **Review Period**
   - Auto-approval for Tier 1 (<$500)
   - Committee review for Tier 2-3
   - Community discussion for Tier 4
   - Decision within published timeframes

3. **Approval/Denial**
   - Clear communication of decision
   - Rationale provided for denials
   - Alternative suggestions when possible
   - Appeal process available

4. **Fulfillment**
   - Direct purchase by DAO (preferred)
   - Reimbursement with receipts (when necessary)
   - Tracking and documentation
   - Thank you and recognition
