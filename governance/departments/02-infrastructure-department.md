## Mission Statement

The Infrastructure Department builds and maintains the foundational systems upon which all other departments operate, ensuring reliability, scalability, and technological excellence across the architecture.

---

## Jurisdiction

### Primary Systems
- **Kubernetes** - Container orchestration and cluster management
- **GKE (Google Kubernetes Engine)** - Cloud-native infrastructure
- **Nova / Athena / Lyra** - Core service constellation
- **WSL / ReflexShell** - Development environment management
- **DreamOS venvs** - Virtual environment systems
- **Monitoring Stack** - Prometheus, Grafana, Elasticsearch

### Oversight Areas
- System uptime and reliability
- Scalability planning
- Infrastructure security (in coordination with DEPT-06)
- Technology stack decisions
