## Department Code: DEPT-10
## Classification: Education & Talent
## Equivalent: Academy + Talent Pipeline

---

## Mission Statement

The Growth Department expands the human capacity of the architecture through education, training, and talent development, building the next generation of contributors.

---

## Jurisdiction

### Primary Systems
- **Intern Programs** - Future contributor development
- **Training Development** - Skill-building curricula
- **Education Portal** - Learning management system
- **Research Publishing** - Knowledge dissemination
- **Curriculum Building** - Educational content creation

### Oversight Areas
- Talent acquisition pipeline
- Training program management
- Educational content development
- Research output coordination
- Career pathway development

---

## Responsibilities

### 1. Intern Program Management
- Design internship structures
- Coordinate intern placement
- Provide mentorship framework
- Track intern progress
- Support transition to full contribution

### 2. Training Development
- Develop skill-building programs
- Create assessment frameworks
- Maintain training materials
- Track competency development

### 3. Education Portal Operations
- Maintain learning management system
- Curate educational content
- Support self-paced learning
- Track learning progress

### 4. Research Publishing
- Coordinate research outputs
- Manage publication process
- Maintain publication standards
- Support academic contributions

### 5. Curriculum Design
- Build comprehensive curricula
- Align with organizational needs
- Update for evolving requirements
- Integrate practical experience
