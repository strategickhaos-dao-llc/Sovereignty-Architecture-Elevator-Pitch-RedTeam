## Mission Statement

The Culture Department preserves and propagates the cultural identity that makes Strategickhaos unique, maintaining symbolic systems, rituals, and the expressive elements that bind the community together.

---

## Jurisdiction

### Primary Systems
- **FlameLang** - Custom symbolic programming language
- **Glyphs** - Visual symbolic elements
- **Sigils** - Protective and meaningful symbols
- **Logos** - Brand identity elements
- **Symbolic Identity** - Overall cultural expression
- **Rituals** - Oath lock, resonance, ceremonies

### Oversight Areas
- Cultural preservation
- Brand identity
- Symbolic systems
- Community rituals
- Aesthetic standards

---

## Responsibilities

### 1. FlameLang Development
- Evolve FlameLang syntax and semantics
- Document language specifications
- Support FlameLang adoption
- Create educational materials

### 2. Visual Identity
- Maintain glyph library
- Develop sigil systems
- Manage logo assets
- Ensure visual consistency

### 3. Symbolic Systems
- Document symbolic meanings
- Coordinate symbol usage
- Protect symbolic integrity
- Evolve symbol vocabulary

### 4. Ritual Management
- Define and document rituals
- Coordinate ritual execution
- Preserve ritual integrity
- Support community ceremonies

### 5. Cultural Preservation
- Archive cultural artifacts
- Document cultural history
- Support cultural education
- Protect cultural identity

|--------|---------|-----------|

| Resonance | Community connection | Weekly |
| Flame Passing | Knowledge transfer | As needed |

### Ritual Elements

2. **Core** - Primary ritual action
3. **Binding** - Commitment or connection
4. **Closing** - Completion and transition
