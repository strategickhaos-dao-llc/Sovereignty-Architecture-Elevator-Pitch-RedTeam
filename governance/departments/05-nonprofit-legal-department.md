The Nonprofit/Legal Department ensures legal compliance, protects intellectual property, and maintains the legal framework of the organization while managing the ValorYield 501(c)(3) operations.

## Amendment Process

### Proposal Phase
1. Department submits proposal
2. Legal review for compliance
3. AI Council technical review
4. Sovereignty constitutional review

### Review Phase
1. 30-day comment period
2. Revision as needed
3. Final legal review

### Ratification Phase
1. Authorized signer approval
2. Documentation
3. Implementation
