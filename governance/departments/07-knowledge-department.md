## Department Code: DEPT-07
## Classification: Information & Research
## Equivalent: Library of Congress + Research Labs

---

## Mission Statement

The Knowledge Department preserves, organizes, and makes accessible all knowledge generated by and for the architecture, enabling informed decision-making and continuous learning.

---

## Jurisdiction

### Primary Systems
- **Obsidian Vault** - 10,000+ interconnected notes
- **GraphView Clusters** - Knowledge visualization
- **Research Vaults** - Specialized research collections
- **Academic Integration** - External knowledge connections
- **Cognitive Pipeline** - Knowledge processing workflows

### Oversight Areas
- Knowledge management
- Research coordination
- Documentation standards
- Information architecture
- Knowledge discovery

---

## Responsibilities

### 1. Obsidian Management
- Maintain the primary knowledge vault
- Ensure consistent linking and tagging
- Manage vault structure and organization
- Support user access and contribution

### 2. GraphView Operations
- Maintain knowledge graph visualizations
- Identify cluster patterns
- Support knowledge discovery
- Enable relationship mapping

### 3. Research Coordination
- Coordinate research initiatives
- Maintain research vault organization
- Support academic partnerships
- Manage research outputs

### 4. Academic Integration
- Connect with external knowledge sources
- Maintain citation standards
- Support academic publishing
- Manage reference libraries

### 5. Cognitive Pipeline
- Process incoming knowledge
- Route information to appropriate stores
- Maintain knowledge quality standards
- Support RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) systems

|------|-------------|-----------|

| Reference | External sources | Per source license |

| Level | Description | Access |

| Public | Open knowledge | All |
| Internal | Organizational | Members |
| Restricted | Sensitive | Need-to-know |
| Confidential | Protected | Authorized only |

## Quality Standards

### Documentation Requirements
- Clear titles and descriptions
- Consistent tagging schema
- Proper linking to related notes
- Attribution and sources
- Regular review and updates

### Knowledge Quality Metrics
- Link density (connections per note)
- Tag consistency
- Update frequency
- Access/usage patterns
- Contribution velocity
