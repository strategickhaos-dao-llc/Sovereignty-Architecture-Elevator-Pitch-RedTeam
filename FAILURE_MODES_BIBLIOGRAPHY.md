# Strategickhaos SAS Failure Modes Bibliography

A comprehensive bibliography of 100 entries analyzing "how this can fail" for the Strategickhaos Sovereign Architectural System (SAS). These references cover failures in DAOs, AI governance, blockchain systems, sovereign AI, and multi-agent systems—providing cross-domain analogies for understanding risks in decentralized autonomous organizations and AI-powered governance.

**Categories are structured around cross-domain analogies:**
- **Physics of Collapse**: Cascading failures and systemic risks
- **Biology of Organisms**: Emergent risks and evolutionary pressures
- **Music of Harmony**: Miscoordination and governance failures

## Cross-Domain Analogies Summary

### Physics of Collapse (Cascading Failures)
- **Entries**: 8, 44, 48, 57, 76 — Critical slowing down, systemic risks, interconnected failures
- **Key Insight**: Early warning indicators precede collapse; monitor for "critical slowing down" in system metrics

### Biology of Organisms (Emergent Risks)
- **Entries**: 21, 37, 67, 81-90 — Emergent behaviors, evolutionary pressures, unintended adaptations
- **Key Insight**: Multi-agent systems exhibit emergent properties not predictable from individual components

### Music of Harmony (Miscoordination)
- **Entries**: 1-7, 33, 74, 88 — Governance failures, voting breakdowns, incentive misalignment
- **Key Insight**: Harmony requires aligned incentives; discord emerges from specification gaming
