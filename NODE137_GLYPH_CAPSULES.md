## Why Glyphs?

- **Verbose**: Too much text, too little meaning

Glyphs solve this:
- **Dense**: One symbol = complex concept
- **Networked**: Explicit relationships
- **Permanent**: Blockchain-anchored
- **Parseable**: AI and human-readable
