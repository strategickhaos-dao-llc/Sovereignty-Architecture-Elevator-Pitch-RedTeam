## ğŸ’³ Payment Processing Setup

### Option 1: Stripe (Recommended for Fast Setup)

**Setup Steps:**
1. Create Stripe account: https://dashboard.stripe.com/register
2. Verify business details (Strategickhaos DAO LLC)
3. Create payment link for each invoice
4. Enable ACH Direct Debit for lower fees
5. Set up automatic receipt emails

**Pros:**
- Fast setup (same day)
- Credit card + ACH support
- Automatic receipts and accounting
- 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (cards)
- 0.8% per transaction (ACH, capped at $5)

**Use for:**
- Small to medium invoices ($1k - $50k)
- Clients who prefer card payments
- International clients

### Option 2: Bank Wire Transfer (Best for Large Invoices)

**Setup Steps:**
1. Verify business bank account
2. Provide wire instructions on invoice
3. Track incoming wires manually
4. Send receipt upon confirmation

**Pros:**
- No transaction fees (or minimal)
- Professional for large amounts
- Direct bank-to-bank

**Cons:**
- Slower (1-3 business days)
- Requires manual tracking
- International wires can be expensive for client

**Use for:**
- Large invoices ($50k+)
- Institutional clients
- Clients with procurement systems

### Option 3: Cryptocurrency (Fastest, Lowest Fees)

**Setup Steps:**
1. Create business wallets (BTC, ETH, USDC)
2. Use hardware wallet for security
3. Provide addresses on invoice
4. Convert to USD immediately or hold
5. Track basis for tax purposes

**Pros:**
- Near-instant settlement
- Very low fees
- Global borderless
- Aligns with sovereignty philosophy

**Cons:**
- Price volatility (use stablecoins like USDC)
- Client must have crypto capability
- Tax reporting complexity

**Use for:**
- Crypto-native clients
- International clients avoiding wire fees
- Fast settlement requirements

### Option 4: Bill.com / Payment Platforms

**Setup Steps:**
1. Create Bill.com account
2. Integrate with accounting software
3. Send invoices through platform
4. Client pays via portal

**Pros:**
- Professional invoice delivery
- Integrated payment tracking
- Multiple payment methods
- Accounting software sync

**Cons:**
- Monthly subscription fee
- Transaction fees
- More setup overhead

**Use for:**
- High volume of clients
- Need for accounting integration
- Professional payment portal
