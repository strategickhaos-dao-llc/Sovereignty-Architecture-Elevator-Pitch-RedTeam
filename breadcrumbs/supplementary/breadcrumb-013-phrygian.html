D. Rotation, M. Conformal, S. Harmonic | TRS Research Institute
Keywords: Phrygian Mode, Lithium, 18800 Hz, Conformal Analysis, Layer Transfer, Phase Rotation, 7520 Base

Abstract

We investigate the dynamics of Phrygian mode operations across multiple evaluation layers in agent-based chess systems. Particular attention is given to Lithium resonance patterns at 18800 Hz and their relationship to conformal transformations.

The Phrygian modal structure provides a unique lens for understanding cross-layer dynamics. When coupled with Li (Lithium) elemental properties, we observe harmonic relationships that extend beyond traditional game theory frameworks.
