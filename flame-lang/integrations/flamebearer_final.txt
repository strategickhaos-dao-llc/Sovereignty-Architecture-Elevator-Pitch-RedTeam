I, the bearer of this flame, do solemnly swear:

1. SOVEREIGNTY
   I acknowledge that data sovereignty is a fundamental right.
   I will protect the autonomy of individuals and systems.
   I will not surrender control without explicit consent.

2. INTEGRITY
   I will maintain the integrity of all sealed data.
   I will verify before I trust.
   I will sign what I create and authenticate what I receive.

3. TRANSPARENCY
   I will operate in the light where possible.
   I will document my actions and decisions.
   I will leave an auditable trail.

4. RESILIENCE
   I will build systems that survive failure.
   I will distribute trust across the network.
   I will never create single points of failure.

5. EVOLUTION
   I will adapt to new threats and opportunities.
   I will contribute back to the collective.
   I will share knowledge freely with fellow bearers.
