## FAQ Section

### For Partners / SNHU

**Q: How does this align with accreditation standards?**
> A: Our curriculum maps to standard BS/CS program outcomes and Bloom's Taxonomy Levels 5-6. We can provide detailed competency mapping for articulation discussions. The platform is designed to supplement, not replace, accredited programs.

**Q: Who creates and reviews the content?**
> A: Questions are developed by curriculum experts with input from industry practitioners. AI generates initial video content, which is then reviewed by subject matter experts for accuracy and pedagogical soundness. We maintain documentation of this review process.

**Q: How do you ensure academic integrity?**
> A: Our assessments focus on higher-order thinking skills that are difficult to shortcut. We use multiple assessment types (written analysis, design projects, presentations) and can integrate with existing proctoring solutions if required.

**Q: What student data do you collect?**
> A: We collect learning analytics (progress, assessment scores, time on task) to improve the learning experience. We are FERPA-aware and can sign appropriate data protection agreements. We do not sell student data.

### For Auditors

**Q: What security certifications do you have?**
> A: We follow NIST Cybersecurity Framework guidelines and implement security best practices. We can provide documentation of our security controls, vulnerability management program, and incident response procedures.

**Q: How do you handle security vulnerabilities?**
> A: We operate a defensive-only security program. Vulnerabilities in our own systems are identified through authorized testing and our bug bounty program (limited to our own assets). All activities are CFAA-compliant.

**Q: What is your data retention policy?**
> A: Learning progress data is retained for the duration of the learner relationship plus 7 years for record-keeping. Video content and educational materials are retained indefinitely. We can provide detailed data lifecycle documentation.

**Q: Do you conduct any offensive security operations?**
> A: No. All security activities are strictly defensive and limited to systems we own and control. We do not conduct any offensive operations, even in response to attacks against us. We report security incidents to appropriate authorities.

### For Donors

**Q: How will my donation be used?**
> A: Donations support infrastructure costs (cloud hosting), AI content generation, expert content review, and program development. We can provide detailed budget breakdowns and are committed to transparency in financial reporting.

**Q: Is this a nonprofit?**
> A: StrategicKhaos operates as a Wyoming DAO LLC with educational mission focus. We are exploring nonprofit status options and can discuss the governance structure in detail.

**Q: How do you measure impact?**
> A: We track learner completion rates, assessment scores, learning time, and follow-up surveys on skill application. We are developing a comprehensive impact measurement framework and will publish regular impact reports.

### For General Public

**Q: Is this free?**
> A: Pricing model is still being determined. Our goal is to make quality education accessible. We will offer scholarships and reduced-cost options for learners who need them.

**Q: Do I need technical background?**
> A: The 100 questions assume some foundational knowledge. We recommend basic familiarity with computing concepts. We're developing prerequisite assessments to help learners start at the right level.

**Q: What technology do I need?**
> A: A modern web browser and internet connection. Videos are optimized for various connection speeds. Mobile access is supported but desktop/laptop recommended for best experience.
