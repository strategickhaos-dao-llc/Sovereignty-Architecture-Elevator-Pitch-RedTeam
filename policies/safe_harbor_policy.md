# Safe Harbor Policy
# StrategicKhaos DAO LLC Bug Bounty Program
# Version: 1.0 | Effective: 2025-11-30

## Overview

StrategicKhaos DAO LLC values the security community and appreciates responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities. This Safe Harbor Policy provides guidelines for security researchers participating in our bug bounty program.

---

## Safe Harbor Promise

StrategicKhaos DAO LLC will not pursue legal action against security researchers who:

1. **Act in good faith** to identify and report security vulnerabilities
2. **Follow this policy** and responsible disclosure guidelines
3. **Do not cause harm** to our systems, data, or users
4. **Report findings promptly** through designated channels

---

## Scope

### In-Scope Assets

The following assets are within the scope of our bug bounty program:

| Asset | Domain/URL | Priority |
|-------|------------|----------|
| Main Website | strategickhaos.io | High |
| API | api.strategickhaos.io | Critical |
| Educational Platform | learn.strategickhaos.io | High |
| Documentation | docs.strategickhaos.io | Medium |

### Out-of-Scope

The following are explicitly **out of scope**:

- Third-party services and integrations
- Physical security testing
- Social engineering of employees
- Denial of service attacks
- Any systems not owned by StrategicKhaos DAO LLC

---

## Rules of Engagement

### Permitted Activities

✅ Testing for vulnerabilities in in-scope assets
✅ Documenting and reporting findings
✅ Proof-of-concept demonstrations (minimal impact)
✅ Suggesting remediation approaches

### Prohibited Activities

❌ Accessing, downloading, or modifying user data
❌ Performing actions that could harm users
❌ Denial of service or load testing
❌ Automated scanning at aggressive rates
❌ Testing out-of-scope assets
❌ Disclosing findings publicly before remediation
❌ Selling or transferring vulnerabilities

---

## Reporting

### How to Report

Submit vulnerability reports to: **security@strategickhaos.io**

Include:
- Detailed description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Impact assessment
- Proof-of-concept (if applicable)
- Your contact information

### Response Timeline

| Stage | Timeline |
|-------|----------|
| Acknowledgment | 2 business days |
| Initial Assessment | 5 business days |
| Severity Determination | 10 business days |
| Remediation Timeline | Based on severity |
| Bounty Payment | 30 days after validation |

## Legal

This policy constitutes our commitment to good-faith security researchers. Researchers who comply with this policy:

- Will not face legal action under CFAA, DMCA, or similar laws
- Will be credited (if desired) for their contributions
- Will be treated fairly and professionally
