## üìñ LEGAL INFORMATION

### Patent Type
**Provisional Patent Application** (35 U.S.C. 111(b))
- Does NOT grant enforceable patent rights immediately
- DOES establish priority date (first-to-file)
- DOES create defensive prior art
- Expires in 12 months if not converted to utility patent
- Not examined by USPTO (provisional apps are filed, not examined)

### Entity Status
**Micro Entity** (37 CFR 1.29)
- 75% fee reduction (from $300 to $75 for provisional)
- Based on gross income limit (~$223k for 2024)
- Must have fewer than 5 previous patent applications
- Must not be assigned to large entity

### Inventor Rights
- Domenic Gabriel Garza retains full ownership
- Can assign to Strategickhaos DAO LLC (qualifies as micro entity)
- Can license to others
- Can sell patent rights
- Must maintain micro entity status for fee reduction

### Confidentiality
- Provisional application remains **CONFIDENTIAL** at USPTO
- Will not be published unless converted to utility patent
- Only inventor and USPTO have access
- Serves as secret prior art that prevents others from patenting
