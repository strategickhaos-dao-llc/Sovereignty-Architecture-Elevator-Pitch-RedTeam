## ‚öñÔ∏è Important Notes

### **Before You File:**
- Search prior art (Google Patents, USPTO database)
- Document your invention thoroughly
- Create detailed drawings/diagrams
- Ensure you qualify for micro-entity status
- Save all documentation

### **After You File:**
- You can legally use "Patent Pending"
- Keep copies of all filed documents
- Track your 12-month deadline
- Continue developing your invention
- Plan whether to file non-provisional

### **Confidentiality:**
- Provisional patents are NOT published by USPTO
- Use NDAs when discussing with potential partners
- File BEFORE public disclosure when possible
