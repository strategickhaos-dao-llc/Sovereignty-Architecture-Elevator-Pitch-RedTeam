## ðŸš€ File Online in 5 Steps

### **Step 1: Create USPTO Account**
Go to: https://www.uspto.gov/patents/apply

### **Step 2: Prepare PDF Documents**
- Save your description as PDF
- Save drawings as PDF
- Combine or upload separately

### **Step 3: Start Application**
- Log into EFS-Web
- Select "Provisional Application"
- Upload your PDFs

### **Step 4: Certify Micro-Entity**
You qualify if:
- Student, OR
- Income < ~$200K, OR  
- Fewer than 4 previous patents

### **Step 5: Pay $65 and Submit**
- Credit card or USPTO deposit account
- Click submit
- Done!

## â° Important Dates

**After Filing:**
- You have **12 months** to file non-provisional patent
- Your filing date is your priority date
- Provisional is NOT examined
- Provisional is NOT published

**Your Options at 12 Months:**
1. File full non-provisional patent (~$400 micro-entity)
2. Let provisional expire (no further action needed)
3. File international application (PCT)
