## Abstract

A comprehensive system combining neurodivergent cognitive patterns with biomimetic swarm intelligence algorithms, governed by the 36 Laws of Impulse. The system includes Impulse Armor technology - a protective framework that shields neurodivergent cognitive processes while enabling enhanced pattern recognition, parallel processing, and collective intelligence capabilities.

## Field of the Invention

This invention relates to artificial intelligence architectures, neurodivergent-optimized computing systems, swarm intelligence algorithms, cognitive protection mechanisms, and distributed decision-making frameworks.

## Background

Traditional AI systems are modeled on neurotypical cognitive patterns, failing to leverage the unique processing capabilities inherent in neurodivergent thinking. Swarm intelligence systems typically lack governance frameworks and protective mechanisms for individual agents. There is a need for systems that embrace neurodivergent cognitive patterns while providing protection and optimization through structured impulse management.
