## AGREEMENT

### 1. ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**1.1 Transfer of Rights**

The Creator hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to the Works, including but not limited to:

- **Copyright:** All copyright interests, including the right to reproduce, distribute, display, perform, and create derivative works
- **Patents:** All patent rights and patent applications, whether filed or contemplated
- **Trade Secrets:** All confidential information, know-how, and trade secrets
- **Trademarks:** All trademark rights associated with the Works
- **Moral Rights:** To the extent permitted by law, all moral rights and rights of attribution

**1.2 Works Included**

The Works include, but are not limited to:
- Sovereignty Architecture design and implementation
- All software code, scripts, and automation tools
- Documentation, tutorials, and educational materials
- System designs, architectural diagrams, and specifications
- All derivative works, improvements, and enhancements
- All works created prior to the date of this Agreement

**1.3 Future Works**

The Creator agrees that any works created in the course of contributing to the Company's projects shall be considered works made for hire or shall be automatically assigned to the Company.

### 2. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

**2.1 Creator Representations**

The Creator represents and warrants that:
- The Creator is the sole and exclusive owner of the Works
- The Works are original creations of the Creator
- The Works do not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights
- The Creator has full authority to assign the Works
- There are no outstanding agreements or obligations that would prevent this assignment
- The Works do not contain any unlicensed third-party code or materials

**2.2 No Implied Warranties**

Except as expressly stated above, the Works are assigned "AS IS" without any warranties of merchantability, fitness for a particular purpose, or non-infringement beyond those expressly stated.

### 3. CONSIDERATION AND CHARITABLE COMMITMENT

**3.1 Consideration**

In consideration for this assignment, the Company agrees:
- To provide the Creator with equity ownership or membership interest in the Company (as separately documented)
- To acknowledge the Creator as the original author in all derivative works
- To maintain the charitable royalty commitment described below

**3.2 Charitable Royalty Commitment**

The Creator acknowledges and agrees that:
- The Company has committed to assign 7% of all net royalties from the Works to qualified 501(c)(3) organizations
- This charitable commitment is irrevocable and perpetual
- The Creator supports and endorses this charitable mission
- The charitable commitment shall not reduce or eliminate any consideration due to the Creator

### 4. FURTHER ASSURANCES

**4.1 Cooperation**

The Creator agrees to:
- Execute any additional documents necessary to perfect the Company's rights
- Cooperate in any patent prosecution or copyright registration
- Provide testimony or assistance in any IP disputes or litigation
- Provide reasonable assistance in transitioning the Works to the Company

**4.2 Power of Attorney**

The Creator hereby grants the Company a power of attorney to execute any documents necessary to perfect, maintain, or enforce the assigned IP rights if the Creator is unavailable or unwilling to do so.

### 5. ATTRIBUTION AND MORAL RIGHTS

**5.1 Attribution**

The Company agrees to:
- Acknowledge the Creator as the original author of the Works
- Maintain appropriate attribution notices in the Works
- Recognize the Creator's contributions in public communications

**5.2 Moral Rights Waiver**

To the extent permitted by law, the Creator waives any moral rights, including rights of attribution and integrity, that cannot be assigned under applicable law.

### 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

**6.1 Confidential Information**

The Creator agrees to maintain the confidentiality of any non-public information about the Company's business, strategies, or plans.

**6.2 Public Information**

This confidentiality obligation does not apply to information that:
- Is publicly available or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement
- Is independently developed by the Creator outside the scope of this Agreement
- Is required to be disclosed by law or court order

### 7. TERM AND TERMINATION

**7.1 Perpetual Assignment**

This assignment is perpetual and irrevocable. The Creator may not reclaim the assigned intellectual property.

**7.2 Survival**

All representations, warranties, and obligations shall survive the execution of this Agreement.

### 8. GENERAL PROVISIONS

**8.1 Governing Law**

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Wyoming.

**8.2 Entire Agreement**

This Agreement, together with any membership agreement or equity documentation, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

**8.3 Amendment**

**8.4 Severability**

**8.5 No Assignment by Creator**
