## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Strategickhaos DAO LLC (the "DAO") is a Wyoming decentralized autonomous organization limited liability company duly organized and existing under the laws of the State of Wyoming; and

WHEREAS, the Members of the DAO desire to amend the Operating Agreement to establish an irrevocable charitable commitment as part of the DAO's core governance structure; and

WHEREAS, this Amendment advances the DAO's mission to create sustainable, community-aligned intellectual property while supporting educational initiatives in the fields of technology, artificial intelligence, and digital sovereignty.

## ARTICLE I: IRREVOCABLE CHARITABLE ROYALTY COMMITMENT

### Section 1.1 Establishment of Charitable Royalty
The DAO hereby establishes an irrevocable commitment to allocate seven percent (7%) of all net royalty receipts from intellectual property owned or controlled by the DAO to qualified charitable organizations as described in Section 1.2.

### Section 1.2 Beneficiary Designation
The initial beneficiary of the charitable royalty is:

**SNHU Ecosystem Fund**  
**EIN:** 92-3456789 (placeholder - to be confirmed)  
**Status:** 501(c)(3) Public Charity  
**Purpose:** Supporting technology education, artificial intelligence research, and digital literacy initiatives

### Section 1.3 Calculation Basis
"Net royalty receipts" means:
- Gross royalty income received by the DAO
- Less: Documented third-party platform fees charged by distribution platforms
- Less: Direct transaction costs (payment processing fees not exceeding 3%)

### Section 1.4 Scope of Intellectual Property
This commitment applies to all present and future intellectual property owned or controlled by the DAO, including but not limited to:
- Software codebases (StrategicKhaos, PsycheVille, TRS, Valoryield Engine, and all derivatives)
- AI models and training datasets
- Documentation and written works
- Audio, video, and multimedia content
- Patents, trademarks, and other registered intellectual property

### Section 1.5 Irrevocability
This charitable royalty commitment is **IRREVOCABLE** and may not be:
- Reduced below seven percent (7%)
- Redirected to non-charitable purposes
- Suspended or delayed except as required by law
- Modified without unanimous written consent of all Members AND an amended filing with the Wyoming Secretary of State

The commitment may be increased above 7% by majority vote of Members, but never decreased.

### Section 1.6 Payment Schedule
Charitable royalty payments shall be made:
- **Frequency:** Quarterly, within 30 days following the end of each calendar quarter
- **Method:** Wire transfer or check to the designated charitable organization
- **Reporting:** Accompanied by a detailed statement showing gross receipts, deductions, and calculation

### Section 1.7 Governance Integration
The seven percent (7%) charitable royalty commitment is hereby incorporated into:
- The machine-readable governance configuration (`governance/royalty-config.yaml`)
- All smart contracts and automated payment systems
- The DAO's operational procedures and financial controls

### Section 1.8 Audit and Transparency Rights
The designated charitable organization shall have the right to:
- Receive quarterly financial statements showing royalty calculations
- Audit the DAO's books and records related to royalty receipts upon reasonable notice
- Request documentation of platform fees and deductions

### Section 1.9 Successor Beneficiary
In the event the designated charitable organization loses its 501(c)(3) status, dissolves, or becomes unable to receive the royalty payments, the Members shall designate a successor 501(c)(3) organization with a similar charitable purpose within 90 days. During the transition period, payments shall be held in escrow.
