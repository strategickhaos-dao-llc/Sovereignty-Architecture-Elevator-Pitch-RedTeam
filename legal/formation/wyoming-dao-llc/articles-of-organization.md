### ARTICLE VII - IRREVOCABLE CHARITABLE COMMITMENT

**THIS ARTICLE MAY NOT BE AMENDED OR REMOVED UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES.**

7.1 **Mandatory Charitable Allocation**: The Organization shall perpetually allocate seven percent (7%) of all gross revenues received to the charitable fund designated in Article VIII.

7.2 **Enforcement Mechanism**: The primary mechanism for this allocation shall be the smart contract identified in Article VI, which automatically splits all incoming funds as specified.

7.3 **Legal Backup**: If the smart contract fails for any reason, the members and managers are personally obligated to ensure the 7% allocation is made within thirty (30) days of receipt of any revenue.

7.4 **Non-Amendability**: This article constitutes a covenant running with the Organization and may not be amended, modified, or removed by any vote of members, regardless of voting threshold or procedure.

7.5 **Dissolution**: Upon dissolution, any assets remaining after payment of debts shall be distributed to the charitable organizations designated in Article VIII.
