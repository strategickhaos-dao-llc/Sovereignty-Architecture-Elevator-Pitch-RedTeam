## INTERNATIONAL CONSIDERATIONS

### Future International Filings:

**Priority Countries to Consider:**
- **Canada:** CIPO filing
- **European Union:** EUIPO filing (covers all EU countries)
- **United Kingdom:** UKIPO filing (post-Brexit)
- **Australia:** IP Australia filing

**Madrid Protocol:**
- File international application through USPTO
- Based on U.S. application or registration
- Designate desired countries
- Centralized filing and management
- Cost savings over individual national filings

**Timeline:**
- Must file within 6 months of U.S. filing to claim priority
- Can file later without priority claim

**Budget:**
- Madrid Protocol: ~$1,000-$2,000 per country
- Individual national filings: ~$2,000-$5,000 per country

**Recommendation:** Evaluate international strategy after U.S. registrations are secured.
