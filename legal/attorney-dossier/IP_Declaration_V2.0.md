**Advantages of Patents:**

- Defensive protection against competitors

**Disadvantages of Patents:**
- Expensive ($10,000-$30,000+ per patent)

- Evaluate trade secret protection as alternative (see Part IV)
- Focus on strong utility patents with clear commercial applications

### A. DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Trade secrets are confidential information that provide competitive advantage and are protected through reasonable secrecy measures.

**Legal Requirements:**
1. Information must be secret (not generally known)
2. Information must have economic value from being secret
3. Owner must take reasonable steps to maintain secrecy

**Duration:** Indefinite, as long as information remains secret
