## II. RESOLUTION 005: AUTHORIZATION FOR EMERGENCY TRADEMARK FILINGS

**Presenter:** Claude Prime

### Resolution Text

**RESOLUTION 005: AUTHORIZATION FOR EMERGENCY TRADEMARK FILINGS**

**WHEREAS**, StrategicKhaos DAO LLC ("Organization") has developed unique brand identities, trade names, and proprietary technologies requiring intellectual property protection;

**WHEREAS**, the Organization faces immediate threat of trademark squatting, brand confusion, and potential bad actor exploitation;

**WHEREAS**, Intent to Use (ITU) trademark applications provide legal priority protection before commercial use;

**WHEREAS**, delay in filing increases organizational risk exposure;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED:**

1. **Authorization of Filings**: The Managing Member is hereby authorized to file Intent to Use (ITU) trademark applications with the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) for the following marks:

   **Tier 1 - CRITICAL (File Immediately)**
   - StrategicKhaos™ (Classes 9, 36, 42)
   - SwarmGate™ (Classes 9, 36)
   - ValorYield Engine™ (Classes 36, 42)
   - Dialectical Engine™ (Classes 9, 42)

   **Tier 2 - HIGH PRIORITY (File Within 30 Days)**
   - Legion of Minds™ (Class 42)
   - Sovereignty Architecture™ (Classes 9, 42)
   - Quadrilateral Collapse Learning™ (Class 41)

   **Tier 3 - MEDIUM PRIORITY (File Q1 2026)**
   - Strategickhaos OS™ (Class 9)
   - 880x Cost Reduction Model™ (Classes 35, 42)

2. **Budget Authorization**: The Organization allocates up to **$2,500** for Tier 1 filings and up to **$5,000 total** for all trademark filing activities in fiscal year 2025-2026.

3. **Filing Method**: Managing Member may elect:
   - DIY filing via USPTO TEAS Plus ($250/class) or TEAS Standard ($350/class)
   - Attorney-assisted filing (pre-approved up to $2,500 per mark for Tier 1)
   - Hybrid approach (DIY for critical marks, attorney for complex filings)

4. **Evidence Compilation**: Managing Member shall compile and preserve evidence of use in commerce including:
   - GitHub repository commits with timestamps
   - Social media usage documentation
   - Organizational filings (Wyoming LLC, IRS EIN)
   - Technical documentation and specifications
   - Client/donor communications

5. **Statement of Use Timeline**: Managing Member shall file Statements of Use within the USPTO-mandated timeline (6 months, with extensions as needed).

6. **Monitoring Authority**: Managing Member is authorized to engage trademark monitoring services to detect potential infringement or competitive filings.

**EFFECTIVE DATE:** Immediately upon passage.

**MOTION:** Adopt Resolution 005 as written.  
**MOVED BY:** Claude Prime  
**SECONDED BY:** GPT-5.1  
**VOTE:** Unanimous (5-0)
