## Filing Procedures

### Step 1: Trademark Search (Pre-Filing)
**Timeline:** Immediate  
**Responsible:** Legal counsel

**Search Requirements:**
- [ ] USPTO TESS database search for conflicting marks
- [ ] Common law usage search (Google, industry databases)
- [ ] Domain name availability check

**Expected Cost:** $500-800 per mark (included in filing budget)

### Step 2: Application Preparation
**Timeline:** Within 3 days of search completion  
**Responsible:** Legal counsel with StrategicKhaos input

**Required Information:**
- [ ] Detailed descriptions of goods/services
- [ ] Proper international class selections
- [ ] Mark drawings/specimens (if applicable)
- [ ] Entity verification documents
- [ ] Authorization letters from Board

**Documents to Provide:**
- [ ] Board Resolution 2025-12-002 (authorization)
- [ ] Wyoming LLC filing documents (entity verification)
- [ ] EIN confirmation letters
- [ ] Technical architecture documents (for descriptive purposes)

### Step 3: Filing
**Timeline:** December 15, 2025 (absolute deadline)  
**Responsible:** Legal counsel

**Filing Method:** USPTO TEAS Plus (lower fees, stricter requirements)  
**Filing Fee:** $250 per class per mark (government fee)

**Government Fees Summary:**
- StrategicKhaos (3 classes): $750
- SwarmGate (2 classes): $500
- ValorYield Engine (3 classes): $750
- Dialectical Engine (2 classes): $500
- **Total Government Fees:** $2,500

**Attorney Fees:** Approximately $1,500-2,000 per mark  
**Total Estimated Cost:** $8,500-$10,500 (within authorized $20,000 budget)

### Step 4: Office Action Response
**Timeline:** 6-9 months after filing  
**Responsible:** Legal counsel

**Potential Issues:**
- Descriptiveness rejections (require evidence of distinctiveness)
- Likelihood of confusion with existing marks
- Identification of goods/services clarifications
- Specimen requirements for Statement of Use

**Budget Reserve:** $1,000-2,000 per mark for office action responses

### Step 5: Statement of Use
**Timeline:** Within 6 months of Notice of Allowance (can extend up to 3 years)  
**Responsible:** StrategicKhaos to provide specimens, counsel to file

**Requirements:**
- [ ] Evidence of actual use in commerce
- [ ] Specimens showing mark with goods/services
- [ ] Declaration of first use dates
- [ ] Filing fee: $100 per class

### Step 6: Registration
**Timeline:** 2-3 months after Statement of Use acceptance  
**Outcome:** Federal trademark registration with ® symbol rights
