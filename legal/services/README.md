## ‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT LEGAL NOTICES

### ATTORNEY REVIEW REQUIRED

**All contract templates MUST be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before use.**

These templates are provided for informational purposes and as starting points for discussion with your attorney. They are NOT legal advice and should NOT be used without proper legal review.

**Why attorney review is critical:**
- State-specific laws vary
- Your specific situation may require modifications
- Contract terms must be enforceable in your jurisdiction
- Liability limitations have legal requirements
- IP protection clauses must be properly drafted

**Estimated attorney cost:** $2,000-$3,000 for complete review

---

### INSURANCE REQUIRED

**Do NOT begin client engagements without proper insurance in place.**

Required policies:
- **Professional Liability (E&O):** $1M-$2M minimum
- **Cyber Liability:** $1M-$3M minimum
- **General Liability:** $1M minimum

Most clients (especially law firms and enterprises) will require proof of insurance before signing contracts.

**Estimated insurance cost:** $5,500-$15,000/year

---

### COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

**Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA) Compliance:**
- Written authorization required for all testing
- Test only authorized systems
- Stay within defined scope
- Document all authorization

**State Computer Crime Laws:**
- Vary by state
- May require additional authorizations
- Consult with attorney on state-specific requirements

**Industry Regulations:**
- GDPR (data protection)
- CCPA (privacy)
- HIPAA (healthcare)
- PCI-DSS (payment cards)
- Comply with applicable regulations for your clients
