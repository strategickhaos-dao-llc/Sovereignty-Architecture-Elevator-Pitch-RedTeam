### 2.3 Exclusions from Confidential Information

Information will NOT be considered Confidential Information if:

1. Information was publicly available at the time of disclosure

4. Information is independently developed by receiving Party without use of Confidential Information
5. Information is rightfully received from a third party without confidentiality restrictions
6. Information is required to be disclosed by law (with prior notice to disclosing Party)

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

Each Party agrees to:

### 3.1 Non-Disclosure

- **Maintain Confidentiality:** Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential
- **Limit Access:** Disclose only to employees, contractors, or advisors with a legitimate need to know
- **Protect Information:** Use same degree of care as used for own confidential information (but no less than reasonable care)
- **Prevent Unauthorized Use:** Take reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized disclosure or use

### 3.2 Use Restrictions

Confidential Information may be used ONLY for:

- Evaluation of business relationship
- Performance of authorized services
- Legitimate business purposes directly related to the Purpose

Confidential Information may NOT be used for:

- Competitive advantage or reverse engineering
- Development of similar products or services
- Disclosure to competitors or third parties
- Any purpose other than the Purpose

### 3.3 Third-Party Disclosure

Before disclosing Confidential Information to third parties, receiving Party must:

1. Obtain written consent from disclosing Party
2. Ensure third party signs equivalent NDA
3. Remain liable for third party's use and protection of information

**Exceptions:** Disclosure to legal counsel, accountants, or other professional advisors bound by professional confidentiality obligations.

## 4. SPECIFIC PROTECTIONS FOR FLAMELANG TECHNOLOGY

### 4.1 Provider's Proprietary Technology

Client specifically acknowledges and agrees that:

- **FlameLang is Trade Secret:** FlameLang source code, algorithms, and methodologies constitute Provider's valuable trade secrets
- **No Reverse Engineering:** Client will not reverse-engineer, decompile, disassemble, or attempt to derive FlameLang source code
- **Limited License:** Client receives only a non-exclusive, limited license to use FlameLang-generated security models for internal purposes
- **No Transfer:** Client will not transfer, sell, license, or provide FlameLang technology to third parties
- **No Competitive Use:** Client will not use FlameLang technology to develop competing security analysis tools

### 4.2 Security Models and Reports

FlameLang-generated security models and analysis:

- May be retained by Client for internal security use
- May not be shared with Provider's competitors
- May not be used to train alternative machine learning models
- Remain subject to this NDA's confidentiality obligations

### 4.3 Enhanced Protection Measures

For FlameLang technology, Client agrees to:

- Store all FlameLang-related materials in encrypted form
- Limit access to authorized personnel only
- Log and monitor access to FlameLang deliverables
- Immediately notify Provider of any suspected unauthorized access
- Return or destroy FlameLang materials upon request or termination

## 5. SECURITY VULNERABILITY DISCLOSURE

### 5.1 Vulnerability Findings

Security vulnerabilities discovered during assessments are considered:

- **Highly Confidential:** Subject to strictest confidentiality
- **Time-Sensitive:** Must not be disclosed until remediation
- **Legally Protected:** May be designated as attorney work product

### 5.2 Responsible Disclosure

Client agrees to:

- Not publicly disclose vulnerabilities without Provider consent
- Allow reasonable time for remediation before disclosure
- Coordinate disclosure with Provider if required
- Comply with responsible disclosure best practices

### 5.3 Public Disclosure Prohibition

Neither Party will publicly disclose:

- Specific vulnerability details
- Proof-of-concept exploit code
- Assessment findings or reports
- Security control weaknesses

**Exception:** Disclosure required by law with prior notice to other Party.
