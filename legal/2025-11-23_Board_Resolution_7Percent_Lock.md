# BOARD RESOLUTION — 7% PERPETUAL CHARITABLE ROYALTY LOCK

**Strategickhaos DAO LLC**

**Resolution No:** 2025-001

## WHEREAS

**WHEREAS,** Strategickhaos DAO LLC (the "Company") is a Wyoming limited liability company organized for the purpose of developing and commercializing intellectual property, including but not limited to the Sovereignty Architecture and related technologies;

**WHEREAS,** the Company has been assigned intellectual property rights from its creators and contributors;

**WHEREAS,** the Company desires to establish a perpetual, irrevocable commitment to charitable giving through a structured royalty assignment to qualified 501(c)(3) organizations;

**WHEREAS,** the Company intends to comply with all applicable IRS regulations regarding charitable contributions, including but not limited to IRC §170(f)(3) regarding partial interest gifts;

**WHEREAS,** the Managing Member has determined that a 7% charitable royalty commitment on net royalties aligns with the Company's mission and values;

---

## NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED

**1. IRREVOCABLE CHARITABLE COMMITMENT**

The Company hereby establishes an irrevocable commitment to assign seven percent (7%) of all net royalties, licensing fees, and other income derived from the Company's intellectual property to qualified 501(c)(3) charitable organizations.

**2. DEFINITION OF NET ROYALTIES**

For purposes of this Resolution, "net royalties" shall mean:
- Gross royalties, licensing fees, and IP-related income
- **Less:** Documented platform fees (e.g., GitHub Sponsors, Patreon, payment processor fees)
- **Less:** Direct costs attributable to IP maintenance and prosecution

**3. INITIAL BENEFICIARY DESIGNATION**

The initial designated beneficiary shall be:
- **Organization Name:** [SNHU Ecosystem Fund or other qualified 501(c)(3)]
- **EIN:** [92-3456789 or actual EIN]
- **Purpose:** Supporting education, research, and community development

**4. ASSIGNMENT MECHANICS**

The Company shall:
- Execute a formal Charitable Royalty Assignment Agreement with the designated charity
- Establish quarterly payment schedules for royalty distributions
- Maintain detailed accounting records of all royalty income and distributions
- Provide annual reporting to the charitable beneficiary

**5. IRREVOCABILITY AND SUCCESSOR DESIGNATION**

This commitment is **IRREVOCABLE** and shall:
- Bind the Company in perpetuity
- Survive any merger, acquisition, or restructuring of the Company
- Pass to successor entities in any transfer of the Company's assets
- Allow the Managing Member to designate successor 501(c)(3) beneficiaries only if the current beneficiary ceases to exist or loses tax-exempt status

**6. TAX COMPLIANCE**

The Company shall:
- Obtain contemporaneous written acknowledgment from the charity (IRS requirements)
- Complete Form 8283 for non-cash charitable contributions as required
- Maintain all documentation for IRS audit purposes
- Obtain qualified appraisals as required by IRC §170(f)(11)

**7. GOVERNANCE AND ENFORCEMENT**

This Resolution shall be:
- Recorded in the Company's machine-readable governance system
- GPG-signed and blockchain-anchored via OpenTimestamps
- Subject to annual review for compliance verification
- Enforced through automated payment systems where feasible

**8. AUTHORITY**

The Managing Member is hereby authorized to:
- Execute all documents necessary to implement this Resolution
- Engage legal counsel to finalize the Charitable Royalty Assignment Agreement
- Establish banking and payment systems to facilitate distributions
- Amend administrative provisions (but not the 7% rate or irrevocability) as needed for compliance
