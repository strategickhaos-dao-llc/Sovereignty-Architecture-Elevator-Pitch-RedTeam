## üèõÔ∏è Formation Steps

### Step 1: Choose and Reserve Name

**Proposed Name**: Valoryield Refuge Foundation

**Requirements**:
- Must include "Foundation," "Inc.," "Corporation," or similar
- Cannot be confusingly similar to existing Wyoming nonprofits
- Check availability at: [Wyoming Secretary of State](https://wyoming.gov/sos)

**Name Reservation** (Optional):
- Fee: $60
- Duration: 120 days

### Step 2: Prepare Articles of Incorporation

**Required Elements**:
```yaml
articles_of_incorporation:
  name: "Valoryield Refuge Foundation"
  type: "Nonprofit Corporation"
  purpose: |
    Organized exclusively for charitable, educational, and scientific purposes
    within the meaning of Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code,
    including but not limited to:
    - Supporting victims of abuse, fraud, and predatory practices
    - Funding cybersecurity education and awareness programs
    - Providing resources for digital sovereignty and privacy protection
    - Supporting anti-abuse charities and victim advocacy organizations
  
  dissolution_clause: |
    Upon dissolution, the assets of this corporation shall be distributed
    exclusively to organizations which qualify as exempt organizations under
    Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code.
  
  no_private_inurement: |
    No part of the net earnings shall inure to the benefit of any private
    shareholder or individual.
  
  registered_agent:
    name: "[Wyoming Registered Agent Name]"
    address: "[Wyoming Address]"
```

### Step 3: File with Wyoming Secretary of State

**Online Filing**:
- Portal: [Wyoming SOS Business Center](https://wyoming.gov/sos/business)
- Fee: ~$25 for filing + optional expedited processing

**Required Documents**:
1. Articles of Incorporation (Nonprofit)
2. Consent to Appointment (Registered Agent)
3. Initial Registered Agent Appointment

### Step 4: Obtain Federal EIN

**IRS Form SS-4**:
- File online at [IRS EIN Online Application](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online)
- Immediate issuance
- Required for 501(c)(3) application

### Step 5: Apply for 501(c)(3) Tax-Exempt Status

**IRS Form 1023 or 1023-EZ**:

**Form 1023-EZ Eligibility** (Simplified):
- Gross receipts ‚â§ $50,000 annually for past 3 years
- Total assets ‚â§ $250,000
- Fee: $275

**Form 1023** (Standard):
- For larger or complex organizations
- Fee: $600
- Processing time: 3-6 months

**Required Documentation**:
- Articles of Incorporation
- Bylaws
- EIN
- Budget projections (3 years)
- Narrative description of activities
- Board member information

### Step 6: Create Bylaws

**Essential Bylaw Provisions**:
```yaml
bylaws_outline:
  article_1_name_purpose:
    - Organization name and principal office
    - Charitable purposes and mission
    - Connection to DiviDen Refinery ecosystem
  
  article_2_board_of_directors:
    - Number of directors (minimum 3 recommended)
    - Terms and term limits
    - Powers and duties
    - Conflict of interest provisions
  
  article_3_officers:
    - President/Chair
    - Secretary
    - Treasurer
    - Duties and responsibilities
  
  article_4_meetings:
    - Annual meeting requirements
    - Special meeting procedures
    - Quorum requirements
    - Virtual meeting allowance
  
  article_5_financial:
    - Fiscal year
    - Audit requirements
    - Check signing authority
    - Investment policy
  
  article_6_amendments:
    - Bylaw amendment procedures
    - Notice requirements
    - Voting thresholds
  
  article_7_dissolution:
    - Asset distribution upon dissolution
    - 501(c)(3) compliance requirements
```
