# LEGAL CONTRACT - STRATEGICKHAOS DAO LLC

## Service Agreement and Terms of Engagement

---

## ARTICLE I - DEFINITIONS

### Section 1.1 Definitions
For purposes of this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings set forth below:

- **"Services"** means the cybersecurity, investigation, and consulting services provided by the Company.
- **"Client"** means any individual or entity that engages the Company to provide Services.
- **"Confidential Information"** means any non-public information disclosed by either party.
- **"Deliverables"** means the tangible work products produced by the Company.

---

## ARTICLE II - SCOPE OF SERVICES

### Section 2.1 Service Categories
The Company shall provide the following categories of services:
1. Private investigation and background verification
2. Cybersecurity assessments and penetration testing
3. Digital forensics and incident response
4. Security consulting and risk advisory
5. OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) research

### Section 2.2 Service Standards
All Services must be performed in accordance with industry best practices and applicable professional standards. The Company shall ensure that all personnel performing Services are qualified and appropriately trained.

### Section 2.3 Service Limitations
The Company shall not provide Services that would require the unauthorized access to computer systems, violation of privacy laws, or any other illegal activity. All investigative activities must be conducted within the bounds of applicable law.

---

## ARTICLE III - CLIENT OBLIGATIONS

### Section 3.1 Information Disclosure
The Client must provide accurate and complete information necessary for the Company to perform the Services. The Client shall not withhold material information that could affect the scope or legality of the Services.

### Section 3.2 Authorization Requirements
The Client must provide written authorization before the Company may access any systems, facilities, or information on the Client's behalf. This authorization must clearly specify the scope of permitted activities.

### Section 3.3 Payment Terms
The Client shall pay all invoices within thirty (30) days of receipt. Failure to pay may result in suspension of Services and shall accrue interest at the rate of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month on overdue amounts.

---

## ARTICLE IV - CONFIDENTIALITY AND DATA PROTECTION

### Section 4.1 Confidentiality Obligations
Both parties shall maintain the confidentiality of all Confidential Information received from the other party. Neither party may disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent.

### Section 4.2 Data Security Requirements
The Company shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect Client data, including:
- Encryption of data at rest and in transit
- Access controls and authentication mechanisms
- Regular security assessments and vulnerability scanning
- Secure disposal of data upon project completion

### Section 4.3 Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach affecting Client information, the Company shall notify the Client within forty-eight (48) hours of discovering the breach. The notification must include a description of the breach, affected data, and remediation steps.

### Section 4.4 Data Retention
The Company shall retain Client data only for the period necessary to perform the Services and comply with legal obligations. Upon Client request or project completion, the Company shall securely destroy or return all Client data.

---

## ARTICLE V - LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

### Section 5.1 Limitation of Liability
The Company's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by the Client for the Services giving rise to the claim. In no event shall the Company be liable for consequential, incidental, or punitive damages.

### Section 5.2 Professional Indemnity
The Company shall maintain professional liability insurance coverage of at least one million dollars ($1,000,000) per occurrence. Evidence of such insurance shall be provided to the Client upon request.

### Section 5.3 Client Indemnification
The Client shall indemnify and hold harmless the Company from any claims, damages, or expenses arising from the Client's breach of this Agreement or provision of false or misleading information.

---

## ARTICLE VI - INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### Section 6.1 Work Product Ownership
Subject to payment in full, all Deliverables created specifically for the Client shall be the property of the Client. The Company retains ownership of all pre-existing materials, methodologies, and tools.

### Section 6.2 License Grant
The Company grants the Client a non-exclusive, perpetual license to use any pre-existing Company materials incorporated into the Deliverables, solely for the Client's internal business purposes.

### Section 6.3 Restrictions
The Client shall not reverse engineer, decompile, or attempt to derive the source code of any software tools provided by the Company. The Client may not sublicense or transfer any rights granted under this Agreement.

---

## ARTICLE VII - COMPLIANCE AND GOVERNANCE

### Section 7.1 Regulatory Compliance
Both parties shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including data protection laws, export control regulations, and professional licensing requirements.

### Section 7.2 Audit Rights
The Client shall have the right to audit the Company's compliance with this Agreement upon thirty (30) days prior written notice. Such audits shall be conducted during normal business hours and shall not unreasonably interfere with the Company's operations.

### Section 7.3 Reporting Requirements
The Company shall provide the Client with regular status reports on ongoing projects. For security assessments, the Company shall provide detailed findings reports within fourteen (14) days of completing the assessment.

### Section 7.4 Escalation Procedures
Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall first be addressed through good faith negotiation between the parties. If unresolved after thirty (30) days, either party may invoke formal dispute resolution procedures.

---

## ARTICLE VIII - TERM AND TERMINATION

### Section 8.1 Term
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until terminated in accordance with this Article.

### Section 8.2 Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate this Agreement upon sixty (60) days written notice. The Client shall pay for all Services performed through the effective date of termination.

### Section 8.3 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days of receiving written notice.

### Section 8.4 Survival
The provisions of Articles IV (Confidentiality), V (Liability), and VI (Intellectual Property) shall survive termination of this Agreement.

---

## ARTICLE IX - GENERAL PROVISIONS

### Section 9.1 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Wyoming without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

### Section 9.2 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements relating to the subject matter hereof.

### Section 9.3 Amendment
This Agreement may only be amended by a written instrument signed by both parties. No waiver of any provision shall constitute a waiver of any other provision.

### Section 9.4 Assignment
Neither party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other party, except that the Company may assign this Agreement in connection with a merger or sale of substantially all its assets.

### Section 9.5 Notices
All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by certified mail, overnight courier, or email with confirmation of receipt.
