4. REPORTING AND PAYMENT OBLIGATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4.1 Payment Schedule
    - Payments shall be made quarterly
    - Due within thirty (30) days after each quarter-end
    - Quarter-end dates: March 31, June 30, September 30, December 31

4.2 Payment Statement
    Each payment shall be accompanied by a statement including:
    - Total gross receipts for the quarter
    - Calculation of 7% assigned amount
    - Allocation among beneficiaries per Section 2

4.3 Audit Rights
    - Beneficiaries may audit books and records upon fifteen (15) days' notice
    - Audits limited to once per calendar year per beneficiary
    - Audit shall be at beneficiary's expense unless material discrepancy found

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. IRREVOCABILITY AND NO RETAINED CONTROL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5.1 Irrevocable Assignment
    This is a present, absolute, and irrevocable assignment. Assignor hereby
    relinquishes and releases any and all right, title, and interest in and to
    the assigned seven percent (7%) interest.

5.2 No Retained Powers
    Assignor retains no power to:
    - Revoke this assignment
    - Redirect the assigned interest
    - Modify the beneficiary designations
    - Reduce the percentage assigned
    - Delay or defer payments due

5.3 Delivery Completion
    This assignment shall be effective and complete upon delivery of executed
    copies to each named beneficiary.
