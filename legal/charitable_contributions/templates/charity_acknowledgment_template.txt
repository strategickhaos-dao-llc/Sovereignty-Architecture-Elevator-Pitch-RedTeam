IRS COMPLIANCE NOTES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DONOR RECORDKEEPING (per Publication 526):

1. Written acknowledgment required for any single contribution of $250 or more

2. Acknowledgment must include:
   - Description of noncash property contributed
   - Whether any goods or services were provided in exchange
   - Description and good faith estimate of value of goods/services, if any

3. Donor must obtain acknowledgment by earlier of:
   - Date tax return is filed, or
   - Due date (including extensions) for filing the return

4. For noncash contributions over $500: File Form 8283

5. For noncash contributions over $5,000: Qualified appraisal required;
   complete Section B of Form 8283

DONEE REPORTING (Form 8899):

If this contributed intellectual property produces income for the charity,
the charity may be required to file Form 8899 (Donee Information Return) to
report that income. This may entitle the donor to additional deductions.
