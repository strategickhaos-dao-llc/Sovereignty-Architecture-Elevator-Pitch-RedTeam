# USPTO Forms

This directory contains United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) forms and related documentation.

## Available Forms

### Credit Card Payment Forms

- **[PTO-2038 Credit Card Payment Form](PTO-2038_Credit_Card_Payment_Form.md)** - Form for submitting credit card payments to the USPTO for patent, trademark, and other fees.

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Security Warning:** Do not submit credit card payment forms electronically via USPTO's patent electronic filing system. These forms should only be submitted by mail, facsimile, or hand-delivery to protect your credit card information.
