## Article 9: Fiduciary Duties (SF0068 Modifications)

### 9.1 Modification of Fiduciary Duties
Pursuant to W.S. § 17-31-112, the members of this DAO LLC agree to modify default fiduciary duties as follows:

### 9.2 Duty of Care
Members shall exercise the care that a reasonably prudent person would exercise in similar circumstances, with consideration for:
- Decentralized nature of the DAO
- Technical limitations of blockchain
- Pseudonymous participation
- Smart contract automation

### 9.3 Duty of Loyalty
Members shall act in good faith and in the best interests of the DAO, except:
- Members may pursue own economic interests
- Members may participate in competing DAOs
- Self-dealing is permitted if disclosed and approved

### 9.4 Algorithmic Decision Making
Members are not liable for decisions made solely by smart contract execution that follow proper governance process.

## Article 10: Liability and Indemnification

### 10.1 Limited Liability
Members' liability is limited to their capital contributions. Members are not personally liable for DAO debts or obligations.

### 10.2 Indemnification
The DAO shall indemnify members and administrators for actions taken in good faith on behalf of the DAO, except:
- Willful misconduct
- Gross negligence
- Intentional violation of law
- Breach of fiduciary duty

### 10.3 Legal Defense
The DAO treasury may be used for legal defense of members and the DAO for actions related to DAO business.
