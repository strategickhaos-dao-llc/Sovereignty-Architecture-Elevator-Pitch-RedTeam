## How to Claim a Bounty

1. **Register**: Run `./join-swarm.sh` to register your research node
2. **Collaborate**: Join the Discord and coordinate with other researchers
3. **Research**: Use the RAG system, compute grid, and resources
4. **Document**: Keep detailed records of your methodology
5. **Submit**: Create a detailed report in `bounty-board/submissions/`
6. **Review**: Mirror-Generals Council reviews and validates
7. **Reward**: Bounty paid upon validation and publication

## Submission Guidelines

- All submissions must be reproducible
- Source data and methodology must be shared
- Peer review or independent validation required for major claims
- Ethical research standards must be maintained
- Open-source results preferred (but not required for full bounty)

## Partial Bounties

Significant progress toward a target earns partial bounties:
- 10% for novel methodology or breakthrough insight
- 25% for major advancement with partial solution
- 50% for near-complete solution pending final validation
- 100% for complete, validated solution

## Adding New Targets

Community members can propose new mystery targets:

1. Create an issue in the GitHub repository
2. Describe the mystery and its significance
3. Propose a bounty amount and deliverables
4. Submit to Mirror-Generals Council for review

Approved targets are added to this board.
