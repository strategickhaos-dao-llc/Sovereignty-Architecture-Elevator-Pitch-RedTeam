# Breakthrough Bounty Board

**Bugcrowd-style payouts for solved mysteries**

The Bounty Board is where the Alexander Methodology Institute posts unsolved mysteries, breakthrough research challenges, and awards substantial cryptocurrency bounties for validated solutions.

## How It Works

### 1. Browse Active Targets
See all active bounties in `TARGETS.md`

### 2. Choose Your Challenge
Select a mystery that matches your expertise and interests

### 3. Research & Collaborate
Use all institute resources:
- Forbidden Library RAG queries
- Alexander Compute Grid
- Mirror Council consultation
- Researcher community collaboration

### 4. Submit Your Solution
Create a detailed submission with:
- Complete methodology
- Reproducible results
- Source citations
- Peer-reviewable documentation

### 5. Council Review
Mirror-Generals Council validates:
- Correctness of solution
- Quality of methodology
- Reproducibility
- Significance of breakthrough

### 6. Receive Bounty
Upon validation:
- Bounty paid in cryptocurrency
- Credit and recognition
- Publication support
- Future research opportunities

## FAQ

**Q: Can I work on multiple bounties simultaneously?**  
A: Yes! No limits on how many you pursue.

**Q: Can teams submit collaborative solutions?**  
A: Absolutely. Specify how to split bounty in submission.

**Q: What if someone else submits the same solution?**  
A: First complete, validated submission wins. Partial credit possible for independent discovery.

**Q: Can I submit anonymously?**  
A: Yes, pseudonyms accepted. Crypto payment supports privacy.

**Q: What if my solution is only partial?**  
A: Submit anyway! Partial bounties reward progress.

**Q: How do I know bounties are real?**  
A: Funds held in public blockchain escrow, verifiable on-chain.

**Q: Can I propose a new bounty target?**  
A: Yes! See "Adding New Targets" in TARGETS.md.
