# 100 Bloom's Taxonomy Questions for BS/CS Education

> **Purpose:** This document contains 100 questions organized by Bloom's Taxonomy levels (5-6: Evaluate & Create) aligned with SNHU standards for Computer Science and Business curricula.

## Usage Notes

- **Bloom's Level 5 (Evaluate):** Questions require learners to make judgments based on criteria and standards through checking and critiquing.
- **Bloom's Level 6 (Create):** Questions require learners to produce new or original work through designing, constructing, developing, and planning.
- **SNHU Alignment:** Questions are designed to support competency-based assessment aligned with SNHU's educational outcomes framework.
- **AI Video Integration:** Each question can serve as the basis for an AI-generated educational video in the KnowledgePod system.
