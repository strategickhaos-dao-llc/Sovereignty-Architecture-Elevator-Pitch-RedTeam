## **3. Integration: How the Tiers Work Together**

### **Hierarchical Development**

The three tiers aren't separate skills‚Äîthey're integrated capabilities that build on each other:

1. **Analyze First**: Before I can evaluate or create, I must understand what exists
2. **Then Evaluate**: Understanding leads to assessment of quality and appropriateness
3. **Finally Create**: Analysis and evaluation inform the design of better solutions

### **Example: Complete Cycle**

When approaching a new project:

**Analyze**: I decompose the problem space, identify existing solutions, map the technical landscape

**Evaluate**: I assess current approaches, identify gaps or limitations, justify why new solutions are needed

**Create**: I design and implement novel systems that address the identified gaps
