# Wyoming DBA Filing – Same-Day Process (2025)

## Filing Instructions

Go here: https://wyobiz.wyo.gov/Business/FilingSearch.aspx

1. Click **"Trade Name"** → **"Register a New Trade Name"**
2. Fill exactly like this:

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Trade Name** | Khaos Bounty |
| **Entity Type** | Domestic Limited Liability Company |
| **Entity Name** | StrategicKhaos DAO LLC |
| **Entity ID** | (leave blank, it will auto-lookup) |
| **Description of Business** | "Cybersecurity research and bug bounty coordination services" |
| **County** | Laramie (or your registered agent county) |
| **Registered Agent** | Your current Wyoming agent |

3. Upload nothing (no signature needed for online)
4. Pay **$25** with card
5. Done — you'll get the certificate PDF within 3–24 hours (often instant)
